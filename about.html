Good Land, The Start of Generational Wealth About Our Founder

Founded in 2015 by Jeremiah Mecham Southwest Landings is a project company of a family man trying to bring financial freedom to his own family and to yours. He has worked many years in the utilities industry starting from the ground up to eventually become a manager. He is willing to put in the work and effort to get you your perfect piece of land.

Jeremiah is also a graduate of GCU, a one time Arizona State fair Finalist and Winner of their annual quilting competition, and is a very caring and dedicated father. He promises to make your land buying experience as easy as possible and treat everyone as if they were family.
